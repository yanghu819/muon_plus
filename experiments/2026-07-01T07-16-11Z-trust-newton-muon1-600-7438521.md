## Decision

Trust-Region Newton-Muon passes the 600-step safety gate: no NaNs, no fallback,
stable memory, and a monotone validation trend from 200 to 600 steps. The result
does not yet prove a full-run advantage because the training script has no
fixed seed and there is no same-budget 600-step default baseline in the local
logs. The next high-information experiment is therefore a 2100-step Trust run,
not a broad Scale/Lagged sweep.
